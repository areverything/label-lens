## Evidence

Sunset Yellow FCF (FD&C Yellow 6) is regulated differently in various jurisdictions. In the European Union, it is authorized but requires a warning label for products aimed at children, as it is classified as a Southampton dye (Reg (EC) 1333/2008 Annex V). In contrast, the United States permits its use as a listed color additive (FD&C Yellow No. 6) without such restrictions (21 CFR 74.706). 

The safety evidence includes a re-evaluation by the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA), which is documented (http://dx.doi.org/10.2903/j.efsa.2014.3765). However, no specific Acceptable Daily Intake (ADI) or IARC classification is provided in the facts. It is important to note that being banned in one jurisdiction or having a hazard classification does not equate to being proven harmful.
