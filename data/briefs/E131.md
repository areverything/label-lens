## Evidence

Patent Blue V (E131, CAS 3536-49-0) is not an approved color additive for food in the United States (FDA) (21 CFR 74 (absent)). There is no regulatory status provided for other jurisdictions, so that information is not yet compiled.

The European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) has established an acceptable daily intake (ADI) for Patent Blue V at 5 mg/kg body weight per day (EFSA re-evaluation, https://efsa.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.2903/j.efsa.2013.2818). There is no IARC classification provided for this additive, and being banned in one jurisdiction does not equate to it being proven harmful.
