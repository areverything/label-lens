## Evidence

1. The regulatory status of sodium benzoate (E211) across jurisdictions is not yet compiled. There are no records available regarding its legal status in any specific jurisdiction.

2. The safety evidence indicates that the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) has established an acceptable daily intake (ADI) for sodium benzoate at 5 mg/kg body weight per day (EFSA re-evaluation, http://dx.doi.org/10.2903/j.efsa.2016.4433). There is no IARC classification provided for sodium benzoate, and it is important to note that being banned somewhere or having a hazard classification does not equate to being proven harmful.
