## Evidence

1. The regulatory status of sulfur dioxide (E220) is not yet compiled across jurisdictions. There are no records indicating whether it is banned or permitted in specific regions.

2. The European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) has established an acceptable daily intake (ADI) for sulfur dioxide at 0.7 mg/kg body weight per day (EFSA re-evaluation, http://dx.doi.org/10.2903/j.efsa.2016.4438). There is no information provided regarding its classification by the International Agency for Research on Cancer (IARC), and it is important to note that a lack of classification does not imply that the substance is proven harmful.
