## Evidence

Brilliant Blue FCF (FD&C Blue 1) is authorized as a food color in the European Union under Annex II conditions, and no child-attention warning is required (Reg (EC) 1333/2008 Annex II). In the United States, it is permitted as a listed color additive (FD&C Blue No. 1), but certification is required (21 CFR 74.101). The regulatory statuses differ in that the EU has specific conditions for its use, while the U.S. requires certification.

The safety evidence includes an EFSA re-evaluation on record (https://efsa.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.2903/j.efsa.2010.1853). There is no IARC classification provided, and no claims are made regarding personal harm. It is important to note that being banned in some jurisdictions or having a hazard classification does not equate to being proven harmful.
