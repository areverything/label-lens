## Evidence

1. The regulatory status of sodium nitrate (E251) is not yet compiled across jurisdictions. There are no records available indicating whether it is permitted or banned in specific regions.

2. The European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) has established an acceptable daily intake (ADI) for sodium nitrate at 3.7 mg/kg body weight per day (EFSA re-evaluation, https://efsa.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/epdf/10.2903/j.efsa.2017.4787). There is no information provided regarding its classification by the International Agency for Research on Cancer (IARC). It is important to note that a regulatory status of being banned or a hazard classification does not equate to being proven harmful.
